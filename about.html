Our Story

Nutrition is more than a meal. It's knowledge, care, and access.

The root is how we think about food — how we teach it, grow it, share it, and understand its place in the world.

35.5% of Indian children under 5 are stunted (NFHS-5). 67% of childhood anaemia is linked to inadequate dietary diversity (UNICEF, 2022). 1 in 3 Indian adults will face a diet-related chronic disease by 2035 (ICMR-NIN).

These are not numbers about food. They are numbers about knowledge — what teachers know, what families believe, what communities value, and what systems reward.

Bodhi Greens was founded on the conviction that this is a design problem, not a resource problem. And design problems require systems thinking.

Education for change

Our Philosophy

Four beliefs that guide every decision.

⚙️

Systems before solutions

We understand the system before designing the intervention. We identify leverage points where small, strategic changes create the greatest long-term impact and focus our efforts there.

📊

Evidence before advocacy

Every claim we make is supported by evidence. We clearly distinguish between what has been measured, what has been observed, and what remains a hypothesis, ensuring transparency in everything we communicate.

💚

Compassion as the destination

Food is more than nutrition—it is a pathway to compassion. We nurture care for oneself, for other people, for animals, and for the natural systems that sustain life.

🌱

Honesty about what we don't know

We are continuously learning. We openly share what we know, acknowledge uncertainties, and integrate monitoring and evaluation into every programme to improve with evidence.

🌿 These beliefs are not just principles—they shape every programme, partnership, decision, and outcome at Bodhi Greens Foundation.

Core Values

The principles behind our work.

These values shape how we design programmes, collaborate with communities and report outcomes.

🌱

Transparency over appearance

We report what is real, including what isn't working. Our transparency page is a genuine record, not a performance.

🤝

Precision over sentiment

We do not claim to be "changing lives" without evidence. Language is accountability. Every claim has a source.

📈

Systems over silos

No programme exists in isolation. Every intervention is designed with its second and third-order effects in mind.

💚

Long time horizons

We make decisions as if we will be here in 50 years. Institutional memory, longitudinal data, and patient strategy.

🌍

Compassion as rigour

Compassion for children, animals, and ecosystems is not a soft goal. It is a measurable, teachable, evidence-backed orientation.

🛡️

Knowledge as a public good

Everything we learn belongs to the public. The Knowledge Hub exists to give away everything we know — freely, forever.
