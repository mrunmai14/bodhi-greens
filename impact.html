An Honest Note

We report what we can verify.

We are an early-stage organisation. The numbers we can claim with confidence are modest. Where we have independently verified data, we say so. Where we have self-assessed data, we mark it with an asterisk. Where we are working from early indicators, we say "emerging." We believe this transparency builds better partnerships than inflated claims ever would.
